# ODE4J Physics engine ported to libGDX

I have sucessfully ported ODE4J to libGDX and is compatible with GWT/HTML builds.  This makes it the only real option in terms of  aphysics engine available for libGDX GWT/HTML backend.

Both ODE4J `v0.4.1` and `v0.5.0` have been ported.
